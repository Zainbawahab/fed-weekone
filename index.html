Pallavi Thampi

Graphic Designer

Bio

Pallavi Thampi is a creative who recently graduated from OCAD university with a major in Graphic Design and minor in English. She hopes to harness language, communication design, and technology in pursuit of a more inclusive and empathetic future. She enjoys book-making, illustration, obsessive typography, and noisy photography in strange cities(Buenos Aires, Paris, London, Florence, New York, Mumbai to name a few).

She has been recognized for her work in the past year by the Registered Association of Graphic Designers; receiving an Award of Specialty from Riordon Design as well as an honorable mention from Taxi Design, as well as by OCAD University; receiving the H.L Rous award for typography. She lives and works in Toronto.
